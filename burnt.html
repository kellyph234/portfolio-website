BurnT

What Makes BurnT Different?

  • An app that feels more like a shopping experience than a classifieds app
  • A chat feature that is robust so a personal number exchange is unnecessary
  • An easy place for sellers to make money and easy place for buyers to shop

The Steps

Competitive Analysis

I took the initial step of conducting a competitive analysis where I was able to pinpoint the patterns that were present in my competitors. From there, I was able to create a list of needs for my own app.

  • Messaging Button
  • Item Upload
  • Account Creation
  • Browsing Items
  • Saved Items

Lo-Fidelity Screens

After taking my must haves from my competitive analysis and translating my ideas to pen and paper, I took to my favorite design tool, Sketch, to bring my designs to life

Getting the Feel of It

After creating my lo-fidelity frames, I crafted the color palette and typography. I wanted to give the app an overall vintage/curated feeling that was soft yet trustworthy.
